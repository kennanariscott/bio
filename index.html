Matty B Raps

Who?

Matty B Raps is a YouTube superstar who makes the best music of the decade. The young prodigy started out making covers of popular songs, eventually evolving to his own music. Those who actively listen to Matty B Raps can only describe his music as the hottest fire in the game.

Where?

Matty B came up from covers of pop-songs. At the young age of Seven, the prodigy came up on the YouTube charts with his cover of "Eenie Meenie" by Justin Beiber. He eventually evolved, making hit songs like "Right Now I'm Missing You" and "California Dreamin'"

The Evolution of Matty B

Even though his views peaked in 2013, Matty B is still breaking records and making new hits. Some of his most recent hits, "808s & Motivation" "Ooh Ooh" and "Already Gone" still get views reaching the millions. Matty B is a very ambitious artist and his style is constantly evolving. Coming from pop covers, he let that style go into his music but now he has a much more defined style. He's constantly experimenting with new sounds, his most recent hit single, "808s & Motivation" dives into the always evolving genre of pop-hip-hop fusion. Matty B is always evolving and probably the most ambitious artist in the game.
